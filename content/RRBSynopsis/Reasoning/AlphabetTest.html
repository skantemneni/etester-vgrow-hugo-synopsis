Introduction

Alphabet test is a test of the sequence of words in alphabetical order as they appear in their order. To arrange the words in alphabetical order, first take the first letter of each word and then arrange the words in the order in which these letters appear in the English alphabet.

Types of Questions

There are three types of questions based on the alphabet test which are generally asked in various competative examinations.

Type 1

Arrangement According to Dictionary

Arranging words in alphabetical order implies "to arrange them in the order as they appear in a dictionary". For this arrangement, first we shall take the first letter of each word and then arrange the words in the order in which they appear in the English alphabet, then take the second letter and so on.

Type 2

Letter - Word Problems

In these type of problems, a word is given and candidates are required to answer question like - a word is given and then they are asked how many letters remain same in its position if they are arranged in alphabetical order. Sometimes, a word is given and they are asked, which vowel or a consonant going to follow it.

Type 3

Alpha - Numeric Sequence

In this type of questions, a jumbled sequence of some letters, numbers and symbols are given. Sometimes only English alphabets from A to Z are given.

Solved Examples

Example 1:

If the following four words are arranged in alphabetical order, which word will come in the third place?

  1. Electric
  2. Elector
  3. Elect
  4. Electrode

Solution:

Arranging the words in alphabetical order,
Elect, Elector, Electric, Electrode
Hence, Electric will come in the third place.

Example 2:

Arrange the given words in alphabetical order and choose the one that comes at the second place.

  1. Bathing
  2. Banking
  3. Backing
  4. Banishing

Solution:

Arranging the words in alphabetical order, we have Backing, Banishing, Banking, Bathing.
Hence, Banishing will come at the second place.
