Introduction

As the name implies, in this type of problems, a sequence is formed with a certain number of words given in such a way that a particular arrangement of the words gives a logical step-by-step completion of the process or the activity described.

Solved Examples

Example 1:

Arrange the following words in an ascending order

  1. District
  2. Village
  3. State
  4. Town
  5. City

Solution:

Ascending order of arrangement is as,
\( Village \rightarrow Town \rightarrow District \rightarrow City \rightarrow State \)

Example 2:

Arrange the following words in a logical and meaningful order

  1. Country
  2. Furniture
  3. Forest
  4. Wood
  5. Trees

Solution:

From the above words, it is deduced that a country contains forests, a forest has trees, trees give wood that is use to make furniture.
