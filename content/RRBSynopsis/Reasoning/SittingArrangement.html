In this chapter, we deal with the questions based on sitting arrangement. We are given few objects and then we are to arrange them based on according to the information provided. Objects are arranged either in row or in a circle on basis of such conditions provided. In other questions, we will also deal with arrangements square/pentagon/hexagon/octagon.

Types of Questions

There are two types of questions which are generally asked in various competitive exams.

Type 1

Arrangement Around a Circle/Square

In such questions, we are given some clues regarding the arrangement. We have to apply these clues on a circle and using these informations, we have to find the solutions to questions associated with them. We also deal with square and rectangle arrangement in this section.

Type 2

Arrangement in a Line

Sometime, we deal with questions, where arrangement is required to be done in a line.
