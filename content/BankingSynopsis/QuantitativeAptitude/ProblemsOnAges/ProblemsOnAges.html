Problems on Ages

These are three type

  1. Present Age

  2. Years ago Age

  3. Hence years Age

Solved examples

Example 1:

The ratio of present age of Asha and Bikash is 2 : 4 the present age of Asha is 30 years. What would be the age of Bikash after 6 years?

Answer:
Asha:Bikash present age ratio is 2:4 and Asha present age = 30 years.
Bikash present age is \( \frac{30 \times 2}{2} = 60 \ years \) .
Bikash age after 6 years is 60 + 6 = 66 years.

Example 2:

The ratio of ages of Amir and Salman is at present 3 : 4, 5 years before that ratio was 2 : 3. What is the present age of Amie and salman.

Answer:
Amir:Salman present ratio is 3 : 4, 5 years before ratio 2 : 3.
\( 5 \times 3 = 15 \ years, \ 5 \times 4 = 20 \ years. \)
the present age of Amir and Salman is 15 years and 20 years.

Example 3:

Suman and Bivash is 50 years old and 70 years old. How many years before was the ratio of their ages 3 : 5?

Answer:
Their ratio was 3 : 5, y years before,
So, \( \frac{50 - y}{70} = \frac{3}{5} \)
5(50 - y) = 3(70 - y) = 250 - 5y = 210 - 3y = 2y = 40 = y = 20 years.

Example 7:

Example 8:

Rahul would be thric in 12 years as like sanjay was 12 years ago, Now Rahul is 6 years senior the sanjay, what is the present age of sanjay?

Answer:
Let sanjay present age is x years.
So, Rahul present age is (x + 6).
(x + 6) + 12 = 3(x - 12)
12x + 72 = 3x - 36
12x - 3x = -36 - 72
9x = 108
x = 12
The present age of sanjay 12 years.

Example 9:

The total age of gangotri, sabir, bibhas is 96 years. 10 years ago, the ratio of their ages was 2 : 4 : 5. What is the present age of bibhas?

Answer:
Let 10 years before gangotri, sabir, and bibhas ages 3x, 4x, 5x years respectively.
Then (2x + 10) + (4x + 10) + (5x + 10) = 96 = 11x = 66 = x = 6.
Bibhas present age is \( (5x + 10) = (5 \times 6 + 10) = 40 \ years \)
The present age of bibhas 40 years.
