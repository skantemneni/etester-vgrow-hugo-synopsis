Number Series

In e number series, some numbers are arranged in a particular sequence. All the numbers form a series and change in a certain order i.e. "A sequence or series is a pattern of numbers which is applicable in different ways."

Type of Series

There are different type of number series, which is based on some of the important rules or order.

Pure Series

In this type of number series, the number itself obeys certain order so that the character of the series can be found out. The number itself may be:

  1. Even Number :
    Ex: 2, 4, 6, 8, 10, ?
    Sol: 12

  2. Odd Number :
    Ex: 1, 3, 5, 7, 9, ?
    Sol: 11

  3. Prime Number :
    Ex: 2, 3, 5, 7, 11, 13, ?
    Sol: 17

  4. Perfect square :
    Ex: 121, 144, 169, 196, 225, ?
    Sol: 256

  5. Perfect Cube :
    Ex: 6859, 5832, 4913, 4096, 3375, ? 2744
    Sol: 2744

Difference Series

Under this category, the change in order for the differences between each consecutive number of the series is found out.
Ex. 13, 18, 28, 43, 63, ?
Sol.
13 + 5 = 18
18 + 10 = 28
28 + 15 = 43
43 + 20 = 63
63 + 25 = 88
Ans: 88

Geometric Series

Under this category, each successive number is obtained by multiplying (or dividing) the previous number with a fixed number.
Ex. 5, 35, 245, 1715, ?
Sol.
Here previous number is multiplied by 7,
hence answer is 12005.

Two-tier Arithmetic Series

Under this category, the differences of successive numbers form an arithmetic series
Ex. 4, 5, 9, 16, 26, 39, 55 ?
Sol.
4 + 1 = 5
5 + 4 = 9
9 + 7 = 16
16 + 10 = 26
26 + 13 = 39
39 + 16 = 55
55 + 19 = 74

Type of Question

There are 3 type of questions usually asked in Banking examination.

  1. Missing Number series.

  2. Wrong number series

  3. Coding decoding series

Missing Number series:

In this type of series one number is missing in given series. Such type of series, find the given Ex. pattern and solved the missing number. What value come in place of question mark (?).
Ex. 6 11 21 36 56 ?
Sol.
(adding 5)
6 + 5 = 11
11 + 10 = 21
21 + 15 = 36
36 + 20 = 56
56 + 25 = 81
Hence, Answer is = 56 + 25 = 81

Wrong number series:

In this type of series one number is odd man out. Which does not follow the sequence.
Find the wrong number in given series:
Ex. 2, 3, 7, 22, 89, 440, 2677, 18740
Sol.
\( \begin{align} 2 \\\\[3pt] 2 \times 1 + 1 = 3 \\\\[3pt] 3 \times 2 + 1 = 7 \\\\[3pt] 7 \times 3 + 1 = 22 \\\\[3pt] 22 \times 4 + 1 = 89 \\\\[3pt] 89 \times 5 + 1 = 446 \\\\[3pt] 446 \times 6 + 1 = 2677 \\\\[3pt] 2677 \times 7 + 1 = 18740 \end{align} \)
So, 440 is replaced by 446

Coding decoding series:

In this type of series firstly one series is given and with the help of first series next number.of second series is find out. Ex. \( \underset{3}{5} \ \underset{(a)}{9} \ \underset{(b)}{25} \ \underset{(c)}{91} \ \underset{(d)}{414} \ \underset{(e)}{2282.5} \)
What will come in place of (c)?
Sol.
\( \begin{align} 5 \times 1.5 + 1.5 = 9 \\\\[3pt] 9 \times 2.5 + 2.5 = 25 \\\\[3pt] 25 \times 3.5 + 3.5 = 91 \\\\[3pt] 91 \times 4.5 + 4.5 = 414 \\\\[3pt] 414 \times 5.5 + 5.5 = 2282.5 \end{align} \)
Similarly,
\( \begin{align} 3 \times 1.5 + 1.5 = (a) = 6 \\\\[3pt] 6 \times 2.5 + 2.5 = (b) = 17.5 \\\\[3pt] 17.5 \times 3.5 + 3.5 = (c) = 64.75 \\\\[3pt] 64.75 \times 4.5 + 4.5 = (d) = 295.875 \\\\[3pt] 295.875 \times 5.5 + 5.5 = (e) = 1632.8125 \\\\[3pt] \therefore \ (c) = 17.5 \times 3.5 + 3.5 = 61.25 + 3.5 = 64.75 \end{align} \)
