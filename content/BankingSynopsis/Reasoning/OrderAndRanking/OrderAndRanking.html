Order and Ranking

Order

Order is known as sequence. For example left, right, top, bottom, ascending and descending

Rank

Rank is known s position in the given sequence.

Type of Rank

There are two types of Rank:

  1. Base Rank: When rank of object is given from end.
    For example - A is 4th base rank from left end

  2. Reference Rank: When rank of one object with reference to another object is given
    For example - A is 2nd reference rank to the right of B

Type of order and Ranking Question

Finding the rank of a person from one side value required to find the rank of a person.

  1. Total number

  2. Rank of same person from another side.
    Formula, Rank from \( \frac{left}{right} \) = Total No - Rank from \( \frac{right}{left} + 1 \)

Example :
In a row of 27 students, X is 14th from right end. Then find the Rank of X from left end?
Solution :
Given: Total no.of students = 27
Rank of X from right end = 14
To find: Rank of X from left end = ?
Formula is,
Rank of X from left = Total number of students-
Rank of X from right +1

\( \therefore \) Rank of X from left = (27 + 1) - 14 = 14
To Finding the total number in a sequence/order.
Values required to find the total number.

  1. Rank of a person from one side.

  2. Rank of same person from another side.

Formula, to find total number is:
Total number = Rank of a person from left + Rank of a person from right - 1

Example :
In a row, X is 17th from left end and 19th from right end. Then find the total number in the given row?
Solution :
Given: Rank of X from left = 17
Rank of X from right end = 19
To find: Total number = ?
Formula
Total number = Rank of X from left + Rank of X from right - 1
Rank of X from right - 1
Total number = 17 + 19 - 1
= 35 \( \overset{ 17th + 19 th - 1}{ \longleftrightarrow } \)

To finding the Mid Number
Values required to find the mid number.

  1. Rank of one person from one end.

  2. Rank of another person from another end.

  3. Total number.

Formula to find Mid Number (Simple case):
Note: Simple Case: When total number is more than the addition of rank from both the end. Then such question is based as Mid number (Simple Case).
Formula:
Mid No. (Simple Case) = Total No. - (Rank of one person from one end + Rank of another person from another end).
Formula to Find Mid Number (Overlap Case):
Note: Overlap Case: When Total Number is less than the addition of rank from both the end. Then such question is based on Mid number (overlap case)
Formula: Mid No. (overlap Case) = (Rank of one person from one end + Rank of another person from another end) - (Total Number + 2)
For Example
In a row of 27 boys, X is 17th from left end and Y is 13th from right end. Then find how many boys are in between X and Y?
Solution : Given: Total number = 27
Rank of X from left end = 17
Rank of Y from right end = 13
To find: How many boys are in between X and Y.
Formula is:
Mid No. = (Rank of X from left end + Rank of Y from right end) - (Total No + 2)
Mid No. = (17 + 13) - (27 + 2)
\( \Rightarrow \) 30 - 29

To Find total Number with Mid Number:
Value required to find total number.

  1. Rank of one person from one end.

  2. Rank of another person from another end.

  3. Mid number.

Both Mid number (Simple Case) and Mid number (overlap case) formula is required to find total number. By using Mid number (Simple Case) fromula, we will get maximum total number and by using Mid Number (overlap case). Formula we will get minimum total number.
Example:
In a row, X is 17th from left end and Y is 11th from right end and there are 4 person are sitting in between X and Y. Then find total number of person in a row?
Solution:
Given: Mid No.4
Rank of X from left end = 17
Rank of Y from right end = 11
To find: Total Number = ?
Maximum total number of person:
Mid no. (Simple Case) = Total No - (Rank of X from left end + Rank of y from right end)
Total No = Mid No + Rank of X from left end + Rank of y from right end.
Then, Total No = 4 + 17 + 11 = 32

Minimum Total Number of Person:
Mid No. (Overlap Case) = (Rank of X from left end + Rank of y from right end) - (Total No. + 2)
Total No = (Rank of X from left end + Rank of y from right end) - (Mid No. + 2) then, Total No = (17 + 11) - (4 + 2)
\( \Rightarrow \) 28 - 6
22

To Finding the Exact Mid:
Value required to find Exact Mid:

  1. Total Number

  2. Rank of one person from one end.

  3. Rank of another person from another end.

  4. Mid Number

\( Exact \ Mid = \frac{Mid \ number + 1}{2} \)
