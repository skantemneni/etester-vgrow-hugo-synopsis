Symbols and Notations

In this chapter the number, letter and symbols are indicate by mathematical sign. You have to use these mathematical sign to balnce the given equation.
Direction
In the following have to identify the correct response from the given premises stated according to following sysbols
Ex.1
If '+' stands for division \( '\div ' \) stands for multiplication \( '\times ' \) for subtraction and '-' stands for addition, which one of the following is correct?

  1. \( 18 \div 6 - 7 + 5 \times 2 = 20 \)

  2. \( 18 + 6 \div 7 \times 5 - 2 = 16 \)

  3. \( 18 \times 6 + 7 \div 5 - 2 = 16 \)

  4. \( 18 \div 6 \times 7 + 5 - 2 = 22 \)

Sol

\( + \rightarrow \div \) \( \div \rightarrow \times \)
\( \times \rightarrow - \) \( - \rightarrow + \)
