Matrix

In this type of queuions. either a \( 2 \times 2 \) or a \( 2 \times 2 \) matrix is formed by a group of figures These figures form sets - either row-wise or columnwise and each of these sets follows the same rule. The candidate is required to analyse each the sets to find out the common rule and then on its basis, the missing figure is to be idenyified from a set of alternative, so as to complete the matrix.
