Sentence Improvement

The technique to attempt sentence improvement questions is a variations of spotting errors. In this type of questions a sentence is given with a word or a phrase italicised, underlined or in blod letters. Under this sentence a number of substitutes are given as alternatives. An examine is required to locate the error and find the correct answer from among the alternatives given. When the alternative has been substituted the sentence become gramatically correct. If no other alternative is required the answer is 'no improvement'

Solved examples

Example 1:

I am tried as I am working since 7 O' clock in the morning

  1. I was working

  2. I had been working

  3. I have been working

  4. no improvement

Solution:

(c) I have been working

Example 2:

When it was dark they decide to put at an inn

  1. put off working

  2. put up at

  3. put out in

  4. no improvement

Solution:

(b) put up at
