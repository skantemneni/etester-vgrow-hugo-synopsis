Rotation of Axes

If the axes are rotated through an angle \( \theta \) in the anticlockwise direction keeping the origin fixed, then the coordinates (X, Y) of a point P(x, y) with respect to the new system of coordinate are given by
\( X = x \ cos \ \theta + y \ sin \ \theta \ and \ Y = y \ cos \ \theta - x \ sin \ \theta \) .

Translation of Axes

The shifting the origin of axes without rotation of axes is called Translation of axes.
If the origin (0, 0) is shifted to the point (h, k) without rotation of the axes then the coordinates (X, Y) of a point P(x, y) with respect to the new system of coordinates are given by X = x - h, Y = y - k.
